Fabrication and Replacement Scope

Common Problems

  • Broken springs, snapped cables, worn rollers, and track misalignment
  • Noisy operation, uneven travel, door binding, and opener force issues
  • Sensor faults, remote connectivity failures, and safety reverse failures
