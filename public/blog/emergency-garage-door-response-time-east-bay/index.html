FAQ

Can this be repaired the same day?

Many spring, cable, roller, sensor, and opener issues can be handled same day when schedule and parts allow. Call 9254094974 with the symptom and door size.

Is this safe to DIY?

High-tension spring and cable repairs are not safe DIY jobs. Sensor cleaning or remote battery checks are lower risk, but lifting-system repairs should be handled by a technician.

What should I tell the technician?

Share the door size, opener brand, what the door does, whether it is stuck open or closed, and any visible broken spring, loose cable, or flashing opener code.
