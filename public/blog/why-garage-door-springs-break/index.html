Why Springs Break And How To Catch It Early

What Causes Spring Failure?

Garage Door springs fail from cycle fatigue, poor balance, corrosion, temperature stress, improper installation, and improper adjustments used to compensate for incorrect springs installed by uncertified technicians. Every open/close cycle slowly weakens spring steel until it fractures.

Early Warning Signs

Look for gaps in the torsion spring, a loud bang in the garage, jerky movement, uneven door travel, or an opener that strains to lift the door.

How To Prevent Sudden Breaks

Schedule periodic balance checks, replace worn rollers, lubricate moving points, and upgrade to higher-Cycle springs for heavy-Use households.

When To Call A Professional

If the door is heavy, crooked, or stuck, stop using it. Operating with a broken spring can damage the opener and create a safety hazard.

Need Professional Help?

For safety-Critical issues or uncertain diagnosis, schedule a professional inspection before forcing operation.
