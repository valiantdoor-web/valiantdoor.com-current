Garage Door Tune-up & Safety Inspection

This page is for homeowners who want a full garage door tune-up and safety inspection to reduce noise, catch worn parts early, and verify that springs, rollers, tracks, hinges, opener settings, and safety sensors are working correctly.

What A Tune-up Covers

Mechanical Check

We inspect springs, cables, rollers, hinges, tracks, brackets, and overall door balance.

Opener Check

We test force settings, travel limits, remote response, wall control function, and general opener strain.

Safety Check

We verify sensor alignment, reversal behavior, and common issues that can turn into unsafe operation later.

Best Time To Book

A tune-up makes sense before a failure happens, after you notice unusual noise, before seasonal weather changes, or when the system has not been professionally serviced in a long time.
