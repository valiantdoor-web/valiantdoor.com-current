Amazon Alexa Support

Valiant Amazon & Alexa App

The Valiant Garage Door Alexa app is built to give customers fast voice access to emergency garage door guidance, broken spring safety steps, service areas, booking direction, and the Valiant phone number.

This page supports Valiant Garage Door customers using Amazon/Alexa, the Valiant website, and 800.com-powered messaging or calls. The voice app does not open, close, or control garage doors. It does not collect personal information. For urgent help, call 925-409-4974.

Emergency Garage Door Guidance

If a garage door is stuck, crooked, hanging by cables, or has a broken spring, keep people clear of the door and do not force it open. Alexa can provide quick safety reminders, but a technician should inspect unsafe doors in person.

800.com Messaging And Calls

Valiant Garage Door may use 800.com-powered phone and messaging tools to receive, route, place, and respond to customer calls and messages. Use calls or messages for service requests, appointment coordination, estimate follow-up, and customer support.

Message and data rates may apply. For policy details, see the privacy policy and terms of use.

Privacy And Use

The Valiant Alexa app is informational. It does not request Alexa profile permissions, does not ask for payment, does not collect personal information, and does not control smart devices. The full customer policy pages are Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Use the app for guidance and contact information. Use the phone number or booking page when you need actual garage door service.
