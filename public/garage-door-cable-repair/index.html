Garage Door Cable Repair And Replacement

Cable Repair Focus

Garage door cable repair usually becomes urgent when one side drops, the door hangs crooked, or a cable comes off the drum. We replace worn or snapped cables with matched hardware, reset drums, and verify safe door balance before the opener goes back into service.

What We Handle

Common Problems

  • Snapped lift cables, frayed cable ends, off-Drum cables, and doors hanging crooked
  • Bent bottom fixtures, loose drums, uneven travel, and sudden door drop symptoms
  • Emergency secure-Close needs, cable slack, and unsafe operation after one-Sided lift failure
