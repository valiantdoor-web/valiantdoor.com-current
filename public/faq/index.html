Frequently Asked Questions

How much does garage door repair usually cost?

Most standard garage door repairs range from $150 to $450, depending on labor time and replacement parts.

What is the cost of spring replacement?

Spring replacement typically ranges from $250 to $700 depending on spring size, cycle rating, and door weight.

How long does a repair appointment take?

Many repairs are completed in one visit, often within 1 to 2 hours for standard issues.

Can I open a garage door with a broken spring?

No. For safety, avoid operating a door with a broken spring because it can damage the opener and create injury risk.

How long do garage door springs last?

Typical spring life is 7 to 10 years or around 10,000 cycles, depending on usage and maintenance.

Do you offer emergency service?

Yes. Emergency scheduling is available for urgent issues such as stuck-open, stuck-closed, or unsafe doors.

Do you repair garage door openers?

Yes. We service motor issues, sensors, remotes, keypad sync, travel limits, and safety reverse settings.

Do you provide estimates before work starts?

Yes. We provide clear, itemized pricing before repair approval.

Which brands do you service?

We service many major residential and light-commercial systems, including LiftMaster-compatible opener setups.

Do you serve cities outside Pleasanton?

Yes. We serve Pleasanton, Dublin, Livermore, Fremont, and San Ramon with route-based availability for nearby areas.
