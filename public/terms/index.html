Website and Alexa app content is provided for general information. It is not a substitute for an in-person garage door safety inspection. Garage doors, springs, cables, tracks, and openers can be dangerous when damaged or under tension.

Amazon And Alexa App Terms

The Valiant Garage Door Alexa app provides informational responses about emergency garage door safety, broken spring warnings, service areas, booking direction, and contact information. If Alexa guidance conflicts with visible safety conditions, stop using the door and call Valiant Garage Door or another qualified technician.
