Partner FAQ

Who is the partner program for?

The program is built for Realtors, property managers, builders, HOAs, and local professionals who need dependable garage door help.

What do partners get?

Partners get clear communication, priority coordination when possible, photo documentation, and direct service routing.

Can Valiant handle emergencies for managed properties?

Yes. Emergency calls are prioritized when a door is stuck open, stuck closed, unsafe, or blocking access.

How do I refer a client?

Use the partner form or call Valiant with the property address, problem, and contact information.
