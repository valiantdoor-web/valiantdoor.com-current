Founder Story

Valentino built Valiant Garage Door to give homeowners a garage door company that explains the real problem before recommending the repair. His background is practical and field-driven: springs, cables, openers, rollers, tracks, safety sensors, commercial operators, and doors that fail under daily use.

The company exists because garage door service can be confusing for homeowners. A door can feel heavy because of a spring, refuse to close because of sensors, grind because of rollers, or stop because an opener is being forced to lift a system that is no longer balanced. Valentino’s approach is to diagnose the system first, explain the risk, and offer the cleanest path forward.

Garage Door Advice From Valentino

Broken Springs

If a spring breaks, stop operating the door. The opener is not designed to lift the full dead weight of the door.

Cables And Off-Track Doors

Loose or snapped cables can make the door crooked, unstable, and dangerous. Do not force another cycle.

Openers

Many opener failures are actually balance, sensor, roller, or spring problems. Diagnose the door before replacing the motor.

Maintenance

A quiet, balanced, inspected door lasts longer and is less likely to trap a vehicle or fail suddenly.
