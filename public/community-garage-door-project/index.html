A Safe Garage Door For A Family In Need Every Year

Every year Valiant Garage Door gives away one complete garage door project to a deserving family in need. The goal is simple: restore safety, dignity, security, and reliable access for someone who truly needs help.

Why Valiant Does This

A garage door is more than a moving wall. It protects the home, secures vehicles and tools, keeps families safe at night, and gives people daily access without fear that a heavy door will fail. Founder Valentino Ramirez built Valiant Garage Door around repair-first honesty, and this annual community project turns that philosophy into direct local action.

Some families delay garage door work because of cost, illness, job loss, or other pressure. When a door becomes unsafe, stuck, or exposed, the problem becomes about dignity and security, not just convenience. Valiant created this program to step in once each year and complete a full project for a household where the impact will be real.

Community Garage Door Project FAQ

How are recipients chosen?

Recipients are chosen from nominations based on safety need, household circumstances, project feasibility, and the impact a safe garage door would make.

Who qualifies?

Families or individuals in the East Bay or Tri-Valley with a genuine safety, security, or access need may be nominated.

When are winners announced?

Valiant Garage Door announces the annual recipient after nominations are reviewed and the project can be scheduled safely.

What is included?

The selected project can include the door, required hardware, installation, basic disposal, and safety checks needed to deliver a reliable working system.
