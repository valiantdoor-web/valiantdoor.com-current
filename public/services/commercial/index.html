Commercial Maintenance & Repair Fixed Plans

Unplanned downtime is expensive. Valiant offers structured commercial maintenance plans that keep sectional doors, rolling steel doors, and overhead operators running at full performance — before a broken spring or failed operator forces an emergency call.

  • Fixed-Rate Repair Visits — Flat-rate pricing for common commercial repairs: spring replacement, cable repair, roller replacement, bottom bar damage, and operator troubleshooting. No surprise labor markups.
  • Scheduled Maintenance Contracts — Periodic inspections, lubrication, balance checks, safety device testing, and wear-part documentation. Available quarterly or semi-annually.
  • Priority Response — Maintenance contract customers receive priority scheduling over standard calls, minimizing disruption to loading operations and tenant access.
  • Documented Service Records — Every visit is documented with findings, parts used, and recommended next steps — useful for property management, insurance, and facilities planning.
